About the School

Where Every Child
Flourishes

Govt. Higher Secondary School Vehil has been a pillar of quality, affordable education for the Vehil community. From Class 8 through Plus Two, our school delivers a strong foundation in academics, sports, arts, and character.

Experienced, dedicated teaching faculty across all subjects
Science and Commerce streams with specialized labs
Well-stocked library with 5000+ books and periodicals
Active NCC, NSS, Nature Club, and cultural groups
Annual sports day, cultural fest, and science exhibition
Government scholarships and free education for all
